Western style desserts

The main raw materials for making West Point are flour, sugar, butter, milk, etc. West Point has a high content of fat and protein, and its taste is sweet but not greasy, and its style is beautiful.

Napoleon

Napoleon is also called Melaleuca Crisp, with egg-like custard sandwiched between it. It tastes fluffy and crisp, and it is broken at the entrance. After one bite, the mouth is full of butter.

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert with coffee and wine flavor. When you eat it, it is fragrant, slippery, sweet, greasy and soft, with texture changes, and the taste is not blindly sweet.

Macaron

Macaroon, also known as Macaron and French buns. Rich taste, crisp outside and soft inside, colorful appearance, delicate and small.

Osmanthus Jelly

Osmanthus Jelly is a delicious pastry made from glutinous rice flour, sugar and honey osmanthus, and a traditional snack with Chinese characteristics. It has a long history and is delicious and refreshing.



Moon Cake

Moon Cake is one of the famous Chinese traditional cakes, which is eaten in Mid-Autumn Festival. Mooncakes are round and round, and they are shared by the whole family, symbolizing reunion and harmony.



Lotus Crisp

A lotus cake made of crispy noodles. It looks like a lotus flower, with clear crisp layer, beautiful appearance, crisp and sweet food, and unique flavor.



Hong Kong style desserts

Hong Kong-style dessert is the representative of Hong Kong's trendy cuisine, which originated from the traditional "sugar water" in Guangdong cuisine, and has been innovated by Hong Kong people. It is not only a historical heritage, but also a product of westernization and innovation, especially in the series of "Mango Banji" and "Tanghui Dessert".

Double-layer Milk

All over Guangdong, Macao, Hong Kong and other places, using ordinary milk as raw material. A bowl of good double-skin milk is cream-like, with white color, delicate and tender texture and sweet and light taste.





Mango Tapioca Pudding

Although sago is not the first in Hong Kong, mango sago is spread through Hong Kong-style desserts. The natural edible value of fruits can be obtained by simply matching mango and sago.



Mango Pomelo Sago

The main ingredients are grapefruit, mango and sago, and the main cooking process is boiling. Mango and grapefruit are rich in vitamins, which is a nutritious dessert.

Make

Ice powder making

Material:ice powder and brown sugar

step1:First, pour boiling water into a large container. The amount of boiling water is determined by yourself. If you want to do more, add more boiling water, then pour a proper amount of ice powder into the container. When adding ice powder, keep stirring, and then wait for it to cool automatically.

step2:Make brown sugar water. Put brown sugar into a pot, add a certain amount of cold water or hot water (the amount of water should not be too large to avoid excessive dilution of brown sugar water), then boil it over low heat, and turn off the heat when the brown sugar water is sticky.

step3:Finally, when the ice powder is cold, take it out, cut it into small pieces, and then pour the brown sugar water into it and stir it evenly, so that a bowl of delicious cool summer drinks will be ready.
